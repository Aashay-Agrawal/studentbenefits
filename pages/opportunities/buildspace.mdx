#  buildspace Overview

Buildspace is an innovative online platform offering hands-on project-based learning in tech-related fields. Participants receive a series of projects with detailed steps for practical skill development.

## Nights and weekends 

buildspace's "Nights and Weekends" program is a 6-week journey where you bring your tech project ideas to life. With expert guidance and potential grant prizes ranging from $25,000 to $100,000, you gain valuable skills and become a "Nights and Weekends Graduate," ready to launch your projects into the real world.

## How to apply 

**1 Application Process:**
   - Start your journey by visiting [N&W](https://buildspace.so/nights-weekends).
   - Complete the application form on Buildspace's platform by providing your details and presenting your one-of-a-kind project concept, along with your strategy for execution.

**2 Selection Criteria:**
   - Buildspace's program is all-inclusive; it welcomes applicants of all backgrounds and skill levels.
   - You don't need to be a coding expert; Buildspace values passion and potential.
   - Pro Tip: Craft an idea that's both unique and easy to convey.

## Benefits

1. **Real-World Project Experience:** Gain valuable, hands-on experience by transforming your project ideas into tangible, real-world applications.

2. **Nights & Weekends Graduate:** Join a distinguished group of program graduates, with graduation ceremonies held in various locations such as San Francisco and Dubai, providing a tangible sense of accomplishment and recognition.

3. **Opportunity for Big Grants:** Compete for substantial grant prizes, ranging from $25,000 to $100,000, to fund and elevate your projects to the next level.

4. **Networking Opportunities:** Connect with passionate individuals and experts in their respective fields, fostering valuable relationships and expanding your professional network.
